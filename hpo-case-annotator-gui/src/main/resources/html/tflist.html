List of transcription factors

The HRMD intends to capture the relationship between Mendelian non-coding mutations and disease. Many of the mutations affect the binding site of a transcription factor (TFBS). We enter data about transcription factors and target genes using the Entrez Gene resource. For convenience, the gene symbols and Entrez Gene IDs of some commonly encountered TFs are shown here.
